Top 3 Movies according to Me!

This are my top 3 movies of all time.

The Notebook

The Notebook to me is a classic romance movie that has
just the right amount of all emotions.

Godzilla vs Kong

I am a big fan of the Titan movies but this is by far one of my favourites.
The fight scenes between Titans, Godzilla and Kong were too good.

Transformers: The Last Knight

This is my all-time favourite Transformers movie as it shows how the Transformers came to earth
and it showcases the friendship of Bumblebee and Optimus Prime.
